FAQ

How does the Milliman PRM analytics algorythm work?
The Milliman PRM Analytics algorythm is an inovative predicitive analytic that provides critical information about each attributed patient. It connects the clinical characteristics of the patient withe the estimated financial value that may be derived from the management of that patient, allowing an entity to strategically select and manage patients for the best return on investment. Each patient population analyzed using Milliman PRM will have unique results. The premise for ranking is that patients, whose healthcare expense volatility a potential for avoidable costs are the highest, should be ranked as a priority for additional care management as opposed to simply the highest cost patients or the highest risk patients.

How does Milliman protect consumer privacy?
Milliman PRM operates under HIPPA, whch establishes the requirements for the disclosure of Protected Health Information (PHI). The strict and comprehensive requirements are part of the legislation and are universally recognized throughout the insurance and healthcare industries. Milliman PRM prides itself on our secure systems and state of the aret infrastructure that keeps consumer data confidential.
