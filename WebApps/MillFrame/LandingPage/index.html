Data analytic capabilities just got stronger by a factor of two

Milliman PRM Analytics® platform employs an innovative predictive analytic that provides critical information about each attributed patient. It connects the clinical characteristics of the patient with the estimated financial value that may be derived from the management of that patient, allowing an entity to strategically select and manange patients for the best return on investment.

Not all provider institutions and payers have the same level of population management skills or outcomes. Being able to pick out the differences, as done by PRM, is important for efficient healthcare management.
