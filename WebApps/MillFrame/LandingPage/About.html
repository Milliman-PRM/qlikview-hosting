About Us

Milliman PRM Analytics® offers industry leading ACO management strategies to the insurance industry. Based in Indianapolis, Indiana, the PRM Analytics team offers patient-specific results. This is an important distinction for the PRM as compared to metrics/factors developed from large databases.

PRM Analytics is part of Milliman, Inc. based in Seattle, Washington. Milliman is among the world's largest providers of acturial and related products and services. Founded in 1947, Milliman is an independant firm with offices in major cities around the globe. We are owned and managed by principals—senior consultants whose selection is based on their technical, professional and business achievements.
